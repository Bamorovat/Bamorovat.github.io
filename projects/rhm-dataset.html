Dataset Overview

The RHM (Real-time Human Activity Monitoring) Dataset is a comprehensive multiview visual dataset specifically designed for human activity recognition in assistive robotics and Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) scenarios. This dataset provides researchers and developers with a robust benchmark for evaluating and developing HAR systems that can operate effectively in real-world environments.

Key Features

  • Multiview Capture: 4 synchronized camera views for comprehensive activity coverage
  • Diverse Activities: Wide range of daily living activities and interactions
  • HRI Focus: Specifically designed for human-robot interaction scenarios
  • High Quality: Professional-grade video capture with consistent lighting and setup
  • Benchmark Ready: Pre-processed and annotated for immediate research use

Applications

The RHM dataset is particularly valuable for:

  • Assistive Robotics: Developing robots that can understand and respond to human activities
  • Smart Home Systems: Creating intelligent environments that adapt to user behavior
  • Healthcare Monitoring: Building systems for elderly care and health monitoring
  • HRI Research: Advancing human-robot interaction through better activity understanding
